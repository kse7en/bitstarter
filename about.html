CoFindr: Who are we?

Why are we doing this? And why should you give us money?

Preorder With Bitcoin

CoFindr is a project by KR and K7

We live in Auckland (New Zealand) where property prizes have been rising astronomically in the last years. It is unaffordable to buy land and build your own house anywhere near the city centre where we both work. The houses that are on the market are expensive, with low standard and often unhealthy.

KR (pronounced kara) is an architect and expert in energy efficient housing. She wanted to create a housing cooperative while still playing in card board boxes and blanket forts.

K7 is an hipstagrapher and commands a small army of servers and people for a living. She wants to walk to work and tries to stay away from bugs and slugs.

Contact us @CoFindr

What is the money for?

The money will be used to build the software behind this website. We also need the help of some experts as well as funds to host the site. After the launch we will most likely charge for some of the features.
